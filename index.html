Statlex: Classic Tangram

The tangram is a dissection puzzle consisting of seven flat shapes, called tans, which together can form various shapes.

The objective is to form a specific shape using all seven pieces, which may not overlap. It was originally invented in China at the ancient times.

You can easily learn to master Tangram through the Arcade mode and then move on to the challenge mode which features 400 uniques puzzles. Once you feel you have become a master at this game, you can also try to perform as many puzzles as possible in a hard mode. Hours of Fun ahead of you.
